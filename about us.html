About Us

Empowering Farmers Through Science & Technology

Our Vision

The Agrometeorological Decision Support System (ADSS) is dedicated to transforming Zimbabwe's agriculture by bridging the gap between science, data, and daily farming practices. We aim to improve resilience, boost productivity, and support smart decision-making in agriculture using real-time weather, climate, soil, and pest data.

Our Mission

To deliver actionable, localized guidance to Zimbabwean farmers through an intelligent platform that integrates meteorological insights, agricultural expertise, and digital technology for improved crop and livestock productivity.
