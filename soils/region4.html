Farming Region Four - Semi-Arid Zone

Soil characteristics

Region Four spans the low-lying parts of Masvingo, Matabeleland South, and southern Midlands. It experiences low and erratic rainfall, making it more suited to drought-tolerant crops and livestock production under semi-extensive systems.

Here's a bit more detail about these soil types in Region Four:

Sandy soils and sandy clay loams :

These soils are generally light-textured, well-drained, but low in organic matter and nutrients. They require careful fertility management for sustained crop productivity.

Luvisols and Cambisols:

Common in this region, Luvisols offer moderate fertility and are suitable for small grains, while Cambisols are younger, less developed soils that respond well to soil amendments and proper moisture conservation.
