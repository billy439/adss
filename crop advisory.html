Region I

Rainfall: High rainfall (>1000 mm/year), cool climate.

Suitable Crops:

  • Tea
  • Coffee
  • Bananas
  • Macadamia nuts
  • Temperate vegetables (e.g. carrots, cabbages)

Advisory: Ensure good drainage and monitor for fungal diseases due to high humidity.

Region II

Rainfall: Moderate to high (750–1000 mm/year).

Suitable Crops:

  • Maize
  • Soybeans
  • Tobacco
  • Wheat (under irrigation)
  • Sunflower

Advisory: Use conservation agriculture practices to retain soil moisture.

Region III

Rainfall: Moderately low (650–800 mm/year), prone to drought.

Suitable Crops:

  • Sorghum
  • Millet
  • Maize (short season varieties)
  • Groundnuts

Advisory: Adopt drought-tolerant seed varieties and early planting strategies.

Region IV

Rainfall: Low (450–650 mm/year), semi-arid.

Suitable Crops:

  • Millet
  • Sorghum
  • Cowpeas
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Bambara nuts

Advisory: Consider intercropping and use mulching to conserve soil moisture.

Region V

Rainfall: Very low (450 mm/year), hot and dry.

Suitable Crops:

  • Livestock grazing
  • Opportunistic dryland crops like sorghum and millet
  • Irrigated vegetables and sugarcane (where possible)

Advisory: Focus on livestock production and irrigation-based farming where water is available.

General Recommendations

  • Use agro-climatic forecasts for planting dates.
  • Integrate pest & disease monitoring systems.
  • Adopt agroforestry and organic soil health practices.
