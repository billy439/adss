Farming Region Four - Semi-Arid Areas

Annual Rainfall: 450mm - 650mm

Region Four covers semi-arid areas including parts of Matabeleland, Masvingo, and the southern Midlands. Rainfall is low and erratic, with frequent droughts and poor soil fertility limiting crop production potential.

Farming in this region relies heavily on drought-resistant crops such as millet, sorghum, and cowpeas. Livestock production, especially cattle, goats, and donkeys, is a key livelihood activity. Conservation farming, mulching, and water harvesting techniques are critical for improving productivity.
