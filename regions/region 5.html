Farming Region Five - Arid and Low-Rainfall Areas

Annual Rainfall: Less than 450mm

Region Five includes the hottest and driest parts of Zimbabwe, such as southern Chiredzi, Beitbridge, and parts of Matabeleland South. Rainfall is extremely low and unreliable, making crop production highly risky without irrigation.

This region is best suited for extensive livestock ranching, especially goats and indigenous cattle breeds. Agro-pastoralism and drought-tolerant forage crops are key, and investments in water harvesting, boreholes, and climate-smart farming practices are crucial for sustaining livelihoods.
